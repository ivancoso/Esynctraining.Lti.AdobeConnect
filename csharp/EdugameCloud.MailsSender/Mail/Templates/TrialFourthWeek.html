I wanted to provide you with best practices for designing engaging content inside Adobe Connect with guidelines for interactions and slide counts in a virtual training session. Virtual training will require more slides to keep the pace moving. Interactions should occur every 3-5 minutes…so you designs will look very different from a classroom session that is done face-to-face. Here are the guidelines I use:

Recommended design tips for slide count and interactions:

Training Duration Recommended Slides
30-minutes 20-30 slides
60-minutes 30-50 slides
90-minutes 60-80 slides
120-minutes 90-110 slides

Training Duration Recommended Interactions
30-minutes 5-6 interactions
60-minutes 10-12 interactions
90-minutes 15-18 interactions
120-minutes 20-25 interactions

With these guidelines as your standard, you can get much more out of your live events by running Crossword Puzzles as ice-breaker activities, assessments and games to get real-time analytics inside your event - and surveys at the end to evaluate the student feedback so you can constantly sharpen the saw and make your training better.
